    Frequently Asked Questions
    How does Ace help with my interview prep?
    Ace equips you with all the tools necessary for thorough preparation and outstanding performance during your interview. We offer exercises to cover topics like how to ask good questions, mock interviews, and personalized feedback so you know how to present really well at your interview. We’ve got you covered.
    What are the exercises for?
    When preparing for your interview, it can be hard to know the ins and outs. Questions like 'What's a standout question?' or 'How do I make a lasting impression amidst competition?' can linger. And then there's the art of storytelling—how do you master that?
    We’ve been there so we know getting answers to these tough topics is the key to effective interview prep.
    How can I use the Ace platform?
    Simple! If you’re a job seeker, simply click Try Ace anywhere on the homepage to give Ace a try before subscribing.
    If you’re a tech recruiter, coding bootcamp, or tech community, book a time with us to see how Ace is a great fit for your organization.
    What type of mock interviews do you offer?
    We offer behavioural mock interviews that cover general, high-level technical, and brainteaser questions to help you answer any question like a pro.
    Plus, by practicing your communication skills, you’ll be better equipped for technical interviews too!
    How many mock interviews can be done?
    Simple: Unlimited! When you use Ace, we don’t limit how many interviews you can do. We know that practice makes perfect, so feel free to do as many interviews as you need to feel prepared, confident, and ready to crush your interview.
